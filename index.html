Tom must once again embark on a dangerous quest. This time he must track down the elusive ghost phoenix Spiros who is haunting the Kingdom of Avantia. Along the way Tom faces many challenges including treacherous terrain, fierce beasts, and cunning enemies. But with the help of his trusty companions, Tom is determined to succeed in his mission and restore peace to Avantia once and for all


Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban" is the third book in J.K. Rowling's iconic Harry Potter series. As Harry returns to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry for his third year he learns that the notorious criminal Sirius Black has escaped from Azkaban. With Black rumored to be after Harry he must rely on his friends Ron and Hermione and his new allies to keep him safe

Diary of a Wimpy Kid is a humorous coming-of-age novel written by Jeff Kinney. The story is told through the diary of middle school student Greg Heffley, who tries to navigate the ups and downs of being a teenager while dealing with family, friends, school, and other challenges


The Great Gatsby follows the mysterious and wealthy Jay Gatsby, who throws lavish parties in his mansion on Long Island to impress his former love Daisy Buchanan. Through the eyes of the narrator we witness the decadence, glamour, and greed of the era, as well as the destructive power of unrequited love and the corrupting influence of money
